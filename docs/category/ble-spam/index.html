BLE spam

Various Bluetooth spam exploit supported by CapibaraZero

📄️ AppleJuice

AppleJuice is an exploit found by ECTO-1A that make tons of fake Apple devices advertsing packets. This packets are recognized by Apple real devices like iPhone or iPad and make them crash due to huge flow of packets that device must elaborate.

📄️ Samsung BLE spam

The Samsung BLE spam attack is discovered by Spooks4576 and act like AppleJuice, it spam tons of fake Galaxy Watch advertisement packets and make phone lag a lot. It is adapted for CapibaraZero from ESP32Marauder

📄️ SwiftPair BLE spam

Swift Pair is a fast pairing tecnology used by Microsoft from Windows 10 1803 and like others fast pairing systems it's vulnerable to BLE spam attacks. Like AppleJuice and Samsung BLE spam, the attack, discovered by Spooks4576, make your Windows PC spawn pairing requests from fake devices.

📄️ FastPair BLE spam

Fast Pair is a fast pairing tecnology used by Google in Android 6.0 and above and like others fast pairing systems it's vulnerable to BLE spam attacks. CapibaraZero's implementation spam only fake Arduino 101 advertisement because it works on every Android version.
